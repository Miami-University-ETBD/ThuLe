Vietnamese Dishes

  • Pho
    Pho is a beloved Vietnamese noodle soup, characterized by its fragrant and flavorful broth, typically simmered for hours with aromatic spices and beef bones. This savory base is then poured over soft rice noodles and thinly sliced meat, often beef, which cooks gently in the hot broth. Garnished with fresh herbs like cilantro, mint, and basil, along with bean sprouts, lime wedges, and chili peppers, pho offers a customizable and deeply satisfying culinary experience that is considered Vietnam's national dish.
